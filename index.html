General & Company

DDNEX® specializes in enterprise digital transformation. Our core services include custom software development, cloud solutions (Google Firebase/AWS), cybersecurity consulting, and high-performance web application development for SMEs and Corporations.

Our headquarters is based in Kedah, Malaysia. However, we serve clients globally, with a strong focus on Malaysia and Southeast Asia.

Services & Development

Timeline varies by complexity:

  • Starter Landing Page: 2–3 Days
  • Business Website: 4–7 Days
  • Business Portal System: 10–14 Days
  • Custom System: 3–6 Weeks (depends on scope)

We primarily build custom-coded solutions using modern frameworks (HTML, JS, Firebase) to ensure maximum speed, security, and scalability. We do not use generic templates unless specifically requested for a basic blog.

Pricing & Payments

No. Our pricing is transparent. The quoted price covers development, deployment, and the specified support period. Optional add-ons (like custom domains or extra pages) are clearly listed and charged separately only if you choose them.

We accept online bank transfers (FPX), local bank transfers, and credit/debit cards via Stripe. Payment milestones (e.g., 50% upfront, 50% on completion) may apply for larger custom projects.

Support & Maintenance

After launch, we provide a warranty period (7–60 days depending on the package) for bug fixes. Post-warranty, we offer affordable monthly maintenance packages to ensure your site remains secure, updated, and backed up.
