DDNEX® Payment & Installment Policy

Last updated: March 2026

At DDNEX®, we offer flexible payment options to help businesses launch their websites and digital systems with ease. Clients may choose between full payment, installment plans, or monthly service plans depending on their budget and needs.

1. Payment Options

Full Payment

Clients who choose full payment receive the lowest available price for the service.

Package Full Price
Starter Landing Page RM399
Business Website RM899
Business Portal System RM3,800

Full payment is recommended for clients who prefer one-time payment and immediate ownership.

Installment Plan (Flexible Payment)

DDNEX® offers installment plans that allow clients to split the total cost into smaller monthly payments.

Package Installment Plan
Starter Landing Page RM39 × 12 months
Business Website RM89 × 12 months
Business Portal System RM349 × 12 months

Installment plans allow businesses to launch their website without paying the full cost upfront.

2. Why Installment Plans Cost More

Installment plans may have a slightly higher total cost compared to full payment.

This is because installment plans include:

  • Administrative processing
  • Payment management
  • Financial risk coverage
  • Payment flexibility for clients

By spreading payments over time, DDNEX® assumes additional management and risk responsibilities.

Clients who prefer the lowest price can choose full payment, while clients who prefer smaller monthly payments can choose the installment option.

Installment plans are not interest-based financing. They are simply a flexible payment arrangement.

3. Monthly Promotional Plans

Some packages offer monthly promotional plans which include ongoing services.

Package Monthly Plan
Starter Landing Page RM29 / month (24 months)
Business Website RM69 / month (24 months)
Business Portal System RM249 / month (24 months)

Monthly plans may include:

  • Hosting
  • Maintenance
  • System updates
  • Security monitoring
  • Technical support

These plans are designed for businesses that prefer lower upfront costs with ongoing support.

4. Payment Schedule

Installment payments must be made monthly according to the agreed schedule.

Failure to complete scheduled payments may result in:

  • Service suspension
  • Limited system access
  • Delayed project delivery

Services will resume once outstanding balances are settled.

5. Ownership & Payment Completion

All systems, websites, and digital assets developed by DDNEX® remain the property of DDNEX® until full payment has been completed.

After full payment:

  • Ownership rights are transferred to the client
  • The website may be migrated to the client's hosting provider if requested

6. Early Settlement

Clients using installment plans may choose to pay the remaining balance early at any time.

Once the full balance is settled, the project will be considered fully paid and ownership will be transferred accordingly.

7. Cancellation Policy

If a client chooses to cancel an installment plan:

  • The remaining balance may become immediately payable
  • Services may be suspended until the balance is cleared

Monthly promotional plans may require minimum contract completion before cancellation.

8. Pricing Transparency

DDNEX® is committed to transparent pricing.

All prices displayed are clearly stated before purchase, including:

  • Full payment pricing
  • Installment pricing
  • Monthly promotional plans

Clients may select the payment method that best suits their financial preference.
