FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

How long does it take to build a website?
For a simple static website, delivery typically takes 3-5 working days. For more complex interactive websites or AI integration, it usually takes 2-4 weeks depending on the scope. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.
Do I need to pay for hosting and domain?
Yes, all websites require a domain and hosting. We offer a free ddnet.my subdomain for the first year with selected packages. For custom domains (.com, .my, .net), costs vary but typically range from RM 50-100/year.
Can I update the website content myself?
For our Portal and Database solutions, yes you'll have a dashboard to manage content. For static HTML websites, minor updates may require our assistance, though we can integrate a CMS upon request for an additional fee.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept FPX online banking, credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), Touch 'n Go eWallet, GrabPay, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through Razorpay (Curlec).
Do you provide SEO services?
Basic SEO (meta tags, sitemap, robots.txt) is included in all our packages. For advanced SEO ranking campaigns, keyword research, and ongoing optimization, we offer custom packages contact us for a quote.
