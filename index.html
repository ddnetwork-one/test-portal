LuvTools ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our tools.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Email address – When you create an account or sign in with Google

  • Tool input data – Text, URLs, phone numbers, uploaded images used in our tools

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Usage analytics (pages visited, features used)
  • Device information (browser type, operating system)
  • IP address (anonymized where possible)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our Services
  • To process your tool requests (QR generation, PDF conversion, etc.)
  • To manage your account and subscription
  • To communicate with you about your account
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse

4. Google Account Data & Authentication

About Google Sign-In Data

  • When you sign in with Google, we receive only your email address, name, and profile photo from Google's authentication service.
  • We do NOT request access to your Gmail, Google Drive, contacts, calendar, or any other Google services.
  • Your Google authentication tokens are handled securely by Firebase Auth and are never stored on our servers.
  • We cannot read your emails, files, or any other personal data stored in your Google Account.

  • User account data is stored securely in Google Cloud Firestore with encryption at rest
