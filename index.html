Our subscription model works like any other monthly service. You choose a plan, pay monthly via Stripe, and receive ongoing services including setup, maintenance, and support. There are no long-term contracts—you can cancel anytime, and service stops at the end of your current billing period.

Setup includes initial consultation, design mockups, development, content population, testing, and launch. We handle everything from domain configuration to making your site live. The scope varies by plan—Starter covers basic sites while Pro includes complex integrations and custom features.
