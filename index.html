Refund Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

General Policy

DDNEX® aims to deliver high-quality services that meet client expectations. All services are considered non-refundable once work has commenced, unless otherwise explicitly agreed in writing.

Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds may be considered under the following circumstances:

  • If DDNEX® fails to deliver the agreed service within the timeline specified in the project agreement, and the client provides written notice.
  • If a service is canceled before any work has started.

Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be issued if a project is partially completed and the client chooses to terminate the project.

Any work already completed, including designs, development, or consultation, will be deducted from the refund amount.

Installments and Refunds

For clients using installment plans, only payments for services not yet delivered may be refunded, subject to deductions for work already completed.

Administrative or processing fees included in installments are non-refundable.

Non-Refundable Items

  • Hosting fees, domain registration, third-party software subscriptions, and any other third-party costs are non-refundable once purchased.
  • Services already delivered, including completed designs, content, or technical implementations, are non-refundable.

Request Procedure

Refund requests must be submitted in writing via email or our contact page.

  • Requests should clearly state the reason for the refund and provide supporting details.
  • DDNEX® will review the request and respond within 7 business days.

Final Decision

All refund decisions are at the sole discretion of DDNEX®.

Any approved refund will be processed using the original payment method or an alternative agreed with the client.
