About Us

Our Story

From a small startup to Malaysia's leading digital agency, our journey has been driven by passion and innovation.

2014

The Beginning

DDNet Pro was founded in a small office in Kuala Lumpur with just three passionate developers and a vision to transform Malaysia's digital landscape.

2018

Regional Expansion

Opened offices in Singapore and Jakarta, serving over 200 clients across Southeast Asia with our growing team of 50+ experts.

2021

Innovation Hub

Launched our R&D division focusing on AI and machine learning solutions, winning multiple industry awards for innovation.

2024

Industry Leader

Recognized as Malaysia's #1 Digital Agency with 500+ successful projects and a team of 150+ digital experts.
